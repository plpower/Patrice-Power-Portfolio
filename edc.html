DISC + Evanston Development Cooperative

My team in the student group Develop + Innovate for Social Change at Northwestern University, was paired with Evanston Development Cooperative as volunteer web developers and UX/UI designers.

Over the course of six months my team built and deployed both EDC's public website and back-end tool for housing searches and price analytics. The front-end designer and graphics lead for my team, I worked on creating EDC's logo (above), imagining EDC's website architechture, and generating a brand book and style guide for the newly conceived affordable housing cooperative.
