building on-campus brand awareness

As a market strategist for Northwestern's student-run marketing agency, Form and Function Marketing, I spent my Fall 2018 helping Viet Nom Nom, a local Vietnamese restaurant, increase their brand awareness on campus. Despite it's prime storefront location and presence in four of our on-campus markets, Viet Nom Nom lacked the reputation held by other campus-favorite Evanston eateries. As a team our goals were to strategize Viet Nom Nom's delivery launch with UberEats and Caviar and to design materials for better brand recognition.
