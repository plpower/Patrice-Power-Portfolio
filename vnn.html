Overview

As a market strategist for Northwestern's student-run marketing agency, Form and Function Marketing, I spent my Fall 2018 helping Viet Nom Nom, a local Vietnamese restaurant, increase their brand awareness on campus. Despite it's prime storefront location and presence in four of our on-campus markets, Viet Nom Nom lacked the reputation held by other campus-favorite Evanston eateries. As a team our goals were to strategize Viet Nom Nom's delivery launch with UberEats and Caviar and design materials for better brand recognition.

Campus Posters

QR Code

QR codes imbeded in flyers and sidewalk posters on campus direct viewers to Viet Nom Nom's website, delivery menu, instagram, etc. I wrote a simple python script that Viet Nom Nom employees can edit and use to make future promotional materials more interactive.
