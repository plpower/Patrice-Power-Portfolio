Overview

Taskfull is a React App I built with on a team during a 5-week project for Agile Software Development, a course at Northwestern University.

Inspired by COVID-19 quarantine, Taskfull connects people in need with willing neighbors to reduce grocery runs. Try Taskfull for yourself!
